Yoot Rules:

Set Up: Each player or team starts with their pieces off of the board.

On your turn, roll the sticks to determine how many spaces to move your piece forward. There are 5 possible rolls, with the X side representing the round side of the stick:

Move your piece forward beginning from start (the orange square). If the opposing player or team's piece is already occupying the space, their piece is bumped off the board and back to start!

If you roll a mo or yut, take another turn!

The first player or team to move their piece all the way around board, including moving their piece past the orange square, wins!
